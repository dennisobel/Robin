# Image credits

## These are stock photos, not the actual properties

Every photo here is generic stock standing in for a real home. That is fine for
a demo, but **it must not ship to a live listings site**: showing a stock house
as if it were the property being advertised misrepresents it to the tenant, and
in most markets falls foul of advertising rules. Replace each file with real
photography of the actual home before launch.

The room shots (`rooms/`) are deliberately shared across all twelve listings for
the same reason ŌĆö they are illustrative, not the rooms in that house.
